# Literature dissection 

## General information 

This repository contains documents to explore data documentation, posters, reports, papers, and other material from the Cheadle Center. 

Our group is looking into the aquatic invertebrate abundance at North Campus Open Space.
The question our group is exploring is: what is the strength of the relationship between water temperature and invertebrate abundance?
